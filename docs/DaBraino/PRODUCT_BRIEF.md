## 2. Product Overview

### What Is DaBraino?
DaBraino is a free mobile educational bingo game for kids (grades K-7). A virtual bingo caller reads creative math clues — word problems, contextual stories, and reverse questions — while kids find answers on randomized bingo cards. Get five in a row and call BINGO!

### Who Is It For?
- **Kids ages 5-12** practicing math facts
- **Parents** looking for educational screen time that's actually fun
- **Tutors and teachers** who want a digital bingo tool for math review
- **Homeschool families** needing engaging curriculum-aligned activities

### What Makes It Different?
1. **13 topics, not just multiplication** — Basic math, fractions, decimals, and US States trivia
2. **AI-powered clue variety** — Not just "What is 7x8?" but word problems, stories, and reverse questions
3. **Authentic bingo experience** — Audio caller, B-I-N-G-O columns, free center space, win verification
4. **Teaching moments** — Wrong answers show the correct solution instead of just "wrong!"
5. **Modern app** — 2025-era design replacing apps from 2011-2013
6. **Zero data collection** — No ads, no accounts, no tracking. COPPA-compliant by design.

**DaBraino Support**

Need help with DaBraino? We're here for you.

**Contact Us**
Email: hello@huybuilds.app

**Common Issues**

*The app won't open / crashes on launch*
- Make sure you're running iOS 15+ or Android 10+
- Try closing the app completely and reopening it
- If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling

*No sound / caller isn't speaking*
- Check that your device isn't on silent/vibrate mode
- Check the in-app sound toggle (speaker icon on the game screen)
- Make sure your device volume is turned up

*My stickers / stats disappeared*
- Game data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall and reinstall the app, your progress will be reset. This is a known limitation of version 1.0 — cloud backup is planned for a future update.

*The app is stuck loading content*
- On first launch, DaBraino downloads educational content. Make sure you have an internet connection for the initial setup.
- After the first download, the app works fully offline.

*How do I reset my stats?*
- Go to the Stats screen and tap "Reset Stats" to clear all game data and start fresh.

*Can I change the difficulty mid-game?*
- Not during an active game. Finish or exit your current game, then select a new difficulty from the difficulty selection screen.

**Device Requirements**
- iOS: iPhone or iPad running iOS 15 or later
- Android: Devices running Android 10 (API 29) or later

DaBraino is built by huybuilds — a husband-and-wife team (software engineer + math tutor) building educational tools for their own tutoring students. Version 1.0 includes 13 topics across basic math, fractions, decimals, and US States trivia.

**Frequently Asked Questions**

**General**

*What is DaBraino?*
DaBraino is a free educational bingo game for kids. A virtual caller reads math clues (or trivia questions), and kids find the answers on their randomized bingo card. Get five in a row to win BINGO!

*What ages is DaBraino for?*
DaBraino is designed for kids in grades K-7 (ages 5-12). Easy mode is great for younger kids just starting out, and Hard mode challenges older students with more advanced problems.

*Is DaBraino free?*
Yes! DaBraino is completely free with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no ads.

*What subjects does DaBraino cover?*
DaBraino has 13 topics across 4 categories:
- **Basic Math**: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division
- **Fractions**: Add, Subtract, Multiply, Divide fractions
- **Decimals**: Add, Subtract, Multiply, Divide decimals
- **Trivia**: US States & Capitals

*Will more subjects be added?*
Yes! We plan to add more trivia topics (world geography, science, history) in future updates.

*What devices does DaBraino run on?*
- iPhone and iPad running iOS 15 or later
- Android phones and tablets running Android 10 or later

---

**Gameplay**

*How do I play?*
1. Pick a topic (e.g., Multiplication) and a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
2. A bingo card is generated with randomized answers
3. Tap "Start" — the caller reads a clue
4. Find and tap the matching answer on your card
5. Get five marked cells in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win BINGO!

*What is the free space?*
The center cell of the bingo card is a "free space" — it's automatically marked for you, just like in real bingo.

*What happens when I tap the wrong answer?*
The cell gently shakes, but it still marks. DaBraino verifies your answers when you get five in a row. If any answer is wrong, you'll see a teaching moment showing the correct answer — then the game continues.

*What are the difficulty levels?*
Each topic has three levels:
- **Easy**: Simpler problems, great for beginners
- **Medium**: Moderate challenge, covers most of the topic range
- **Hard**: Full range of problems for advanced practice

*Does the app work offline?*
Yes! After the first launch (which downloads the content), DaBraino works completely offline. No internet needed to play.

---

**Rewards & Progress**

*What are stickers?*
Every time you win a bingo game, you earn a fun sticker! Collect them all in your sticker gallery. There are 30+ unique stickers to earn.

*How do streaks work?*
DaBraino tracks consecutive wins. Your current streak is displayed on the home screen. Keep winning to build your streak!

*Can I see my game stats?*
Yes! The Stats screen shows your total games played, wins, accuracy, and more — broken down by topic and difficulty.

*What happens if I uninstall the app?*
All game data (stats, stickers, progress) is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data will be lost. Cloud backup is planned for a future update.

---

**Privacy & Safety**

*Does DaBraino collect any data about my child?*
No. DaBraino collects zero personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

*Does DaBraino have ads?*
No. DaBraino has no ads and never will show ads to children.

*Does DaBraino have in-app purchases?*
No. Everything in DaBraino is free.

*Is DaBraino COPPA-compliant?*
Yes. DaBraino fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. We do not collect any personal information from children (or anyone).

*Does the app connect to the internet?*
Only on first launch to download educational content. After that, the app works fully offline. The content download is anonymous — no personal data is sent.

*Where is my child's game data stored?*
All data (stats, stickers, settings) is stored locally on the device only. It never leaves the device and is never sent to any server.

**Troubleshooting**

*The caller isn't speaking*
Check that your device isn't on silent mode, your volume is up, and the in-app sound toggle is on (speaker icon during gameplay).

*The app seems slow or laggy*
Try closing other apps running in the background. DaBraino is optimized to run at 60fps on most devices.
